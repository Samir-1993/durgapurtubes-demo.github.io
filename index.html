About Company
Manufacturer and supplier of Oxygen Lancing Tubes And Oxygen Lancing Pipes

We are a leading manufacturer and supplier specializing in high-quality steel pipes, tubes, and oxygen lancing solutions. With a commitment to excellence, our products are trusted by industries worldwide for their reliability and performance. Count on us for your industrial needs, delivering precision and durability every time.

DURGAPUR TUBES PVT. LIMITED, located in ADDA Industrial Area Durgapur, West Bengal, India, operates from a spacious factory spanning 7,000 sq. meters with a 20,000 sq.ft factory shed. We enjoy uninterrupted power and water supply courtesy of Durgapur Projects Limited. Our proficient team of engineers, supervisors, and commercial managers efficiently manage the factory and office.

With a legacy spanning 35 years since our establishment in 1988, DURGAPUR TUBES PVT. LIMITED holds a prominent position as a market leader in Oxygen Lancing Tubes. Our unwavering commitment to quality has earned us customers nationwide, from Tamilnadu in the South to Kashmir in the North and Gujarat in the West to Arunachal in the North East. Moreover, we export our Lancing Tubes to Africa, the Middle East, Bhutan, and Europe, serving customers across the globe.
