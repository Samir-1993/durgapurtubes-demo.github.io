Quality

Quality Assurance

The company is committed to delivering top-notch quality while maintaining affordability, always striving to uphold exceptional standards in every aspect of our operations.

At our company, we’ve always stayed ahead of the curve, ensuring that we have ample installation capacity to meet the ever-increasing demand for our products. In line with our commitment to continuous improvement, we are embarking on an ambitious expansion and modernization project. This initiative is geared towards not only increasing our production capacity but also enhancing product quality and driving down costs, all to better serve our valued customers

Our key features are
  • Pipes are randomly batch-tested for physical parameters, such as mass, diameter, length & surface finish
  • Each and every pipe is subject to a Hydraulic test & Random samples are tested at 750 PSI for long durations
  • Random Samples are drawn and subjected to Bend test at 90°C and 180°C in cold conditions
  • Random samples are also subjected to Tensile and elongation tests
  • Expansion tests for sockets are also carried out
